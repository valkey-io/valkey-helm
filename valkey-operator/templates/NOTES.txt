The valkey-operator has been installed.

To verify the operator is running:

  kubectl get pods -n {{ .Release.Namespace }} -l app.kubernetes.io/instance={{ .Release.Name }}

To create a ValkeyCluster:

kubectl apply -f - <<EOF
apiVersion: valkey.io/v1alpha1
kind: ValkeyCluster
metadata:
  name: my-cluster
spec:
  shards: 3
  replicas: 1
EOF
